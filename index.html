About Us

Founded on a legacy of craftsmanship, Lethabong Glass & Aluminium began as a home-grown business rooted in family tradition. Under my father’s mentorship, I mastered the art of the trade—from traditional putty glazing to modern aluminium fabrication.

He taught me that true quality means flawless execution and lasting durability. Today, we carry that commitment forward, ensuring every project we touch is built to look beautiful and withstand the elements.

Lethabo Mhlanga

CEO & Master Fabricator

Directing operations with a deep commitment to structural integrity, custom design innovations, and absolute client satisfaction across Gauteng.

Our Services
